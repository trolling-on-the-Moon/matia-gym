ABOUT ME

When did I start getting into health & fitness?

It all started at the young age of 5. I began playing competitive hockey and eventually got drafted to the NHL by the Pittsburgh Penguins at age 18. With my previous career involving countless hours in the gym, I turned my attention to the aesthetic side of training. I focused on growing my clothing company which taught me the importance of living an overall balanced lifestyle and brought me to where I am today. As a fully qualified trainer with over 15 years of gym experience, I now focus on helping clients achieve their fitness goals through a new mindset of positivity and determination. Whether it’s to lose fat, build muscle, or improve their general health, we can start a fitness journey together today.

What makes me unique to work with?

My approach to training isn’t just working out. As a former professional athlete who suffered a sudden career-ending injury, I know what it’s like to feel completely lost and at rock bottom. After my injury, I had no direction in life. Battling depression for a long time, I knew that the only person who was going to change that was myself and that I could achieve anything I put my mind to. With this mentality and consistent hard work, I changed my life. For this reason, I feel like I can connect and relate to anyone to help them achieve their goals. Whether it’s to have more confidence to ask someone you’re interested in out on a date or simply feel good about yourself when looking into the mirror. Training helps with so much more than just your body. If I was able to do it, you could too.
